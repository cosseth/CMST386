How to Take Care of Your Flowers

Published: December 4, 2025

Article Summary

Fresh flowers bring joy and beauty into any space, but keeping them vibrant requires a little care. In this article, we’ll share simple tips to help your blooms last longer and stay stunning. Perfect for anyone who wants their bouquet to shine day after day.

Flower Care Basics

  • Trim the stems: Cut 1–2 inches off at a diagonal before placing them in water.
  • Use clean water: Change the vase water every 2–3 days to prevent bacteria buildup.
  • Remove leaves below water line: Leaves in water can rot and shorten the life of your bouquet.
  • Keep them cool: Place flowers away from direct sunlight, heaters, or drafts.
  • Feed them: Add flower food to the water, or use a homemade mix of sugar and lemon juice.
