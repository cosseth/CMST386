About Our Flower Boutique

Meet Adriana

Adriana's Flowers began in 2022, born from the heart of a first-time mom who wanted to nurture her child while following her dream of creating beautiful floral designs.

I craft floral arrangements that represent love, joy in celebrating birthdays, promotions at work, and even sympathy during difficult times.

Proudly serving Hyattsville, MD, and surrounding areas, and also offer home delivery. Our promise is fresh roses and thoughtfully designed arrangements to bring joy, beauty, and heartfelt meaning to every occasion.
